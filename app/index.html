Here’s how we got here…

The land Elk View Estate sits on was purchased from Dick Burhan, a since expired local artist to the Snoqualmie Valley. Susan Loise Blodgett Lenssen and George Sherman Peck III met at Montana State University in rustic Bozeman Montana. Susie's parents where professors there and George's family has roots in the Big Hole River Valley. Our grandfather bought The Trapper Creek Ranch in Melrose, Montana in 1967 and George's cousin JM Peck still runs it today.

The Montana ranch provided livestock for the feed lot at the Peck Farm in Geneva, Illinos. George Peck Sr. instead of selling out to developers decided to sell the farm for pennies on the dollar to Kane County and you can visit Peck Farm Peck today. George Sherman Peck Jr. passed on portions of the inheritance to George III in 2016 and George and Susie used the money to purchace the Elk View Estate.

Elk View Estate construction begin in 2020. George III in addition to being and engineer is also an artist with a degree from Kansas City Art Institute in Sculpture. George and Susie designed their home, and also did the general contracting as well. Building in King County has been a nightmare fought with neighbor lawsuits and continually changing requirements from King County Permitting. They approved plans that were not fealsible and today have yet to give the young couple their certificate of occupancy even though the house has been completed for 3 years. George and Susie need help and your patronage help them get through this difficult time.

Our Features

We’ll Watch the Weather

Download our app and we’ll send you a notice if it’s about to rain in the next 20 minutes around your current location. A good rain can be refreshing, but sometimes you just want to stay dry.

Global Guides

We’ve scoured the entire planet for the best retreats and beautiful vistas. If there’s a corner of the world you want to escape to we know the most scenic and inspiring locations.

Wi-Fi Waypoints

We only send you on trips to places we can personally vouch for as being amazing. Which means we’ve mapped out where local wi-fi spots are and marked them in our app’s map view.

Survival Kit

Everytime you book an escape with us we send you a survival kit with the finest materials. The kit will allow you to setup a tent, start a fire, scratch your own back and lower your taxes.
